The Rules

You will start out with 10 guesses. Each correct guess will automatically show up in the blank spaces. Incorrect letters will appear in the wrong guesses section. If, at the end of a round, you have all the blanks filled, you win. If not, then you lose.

Press any key to start!

Keep in mind that the hints are also random, so will not match the breed, unless you get really lucky.
